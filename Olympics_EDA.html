In this session we are going to take a look at relation between two variables

Number of Male and Female athlete over the years

We are going to plot how the number of male and female athlete participation changed over the year

In the first Olympics games we have no women athlete. But after that we see a grdual increae in women athlete It is very low in the begining and then we see a big rise in 80s.

We also have some missin games 1916 summer, 1940-1944 summer and winter. These are the times of World wars and the games were cancelled.

Also there is a shoft in years of winter olympics. In 1994 the winter olympic year moved to 2 years after summer oympics. And before 1924 we didnt have winter olympics.

We see that the gap in number of male and female athletes in descresing over the years.

Number of countries particiapted

Over the years games saw an increse in number of countries. But in year 1980 we see a shape decline in summer games. this is the same decline we saw in number of athletes too.

This is because the Soviet Union invaded Afghanistan and 1980 games were held in Soviet Union. United States initiated the boycot of games as a protest against invasion.
